Fun Facts about Uranus

Uranus is unique because it rotates on its side! While most planets spin like tops, Uranus rolls around like a barrel. Isn't that wild?

One amazing fact is that Uranus has the coldest atmosphere of any planet in the solar system, with temperatures dropping to a bone-chilling -224°C (-371°F)!

And here's something cool — Uranus has beautiful rings, though they're much fainter and thinner compared to Saturn's. There are 13 rings in total!
