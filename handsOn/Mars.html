Fun Facts about Mars

Mars is known as the "Red Planet" because of its reddish appearance, which comes from iron oxide (basically rust) on its surface. Pretty cool, right?

One awesome fact is that Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system, called Olympus Mons. It's about three times taller than Mount Everest!
