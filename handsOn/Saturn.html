Fun Facts about Saturn

Saturn is a giant planet that’s mostly yellow and brown, known for its stunning rings made of ice and rock. It’s the second-largest planet in our solar system, and it looks very beautiful from far away!

One fun fact about Saturn is that it’s a gas giant, which means it doesn’t have a solid surface like Earth. It’s very cold up in the clouds, with temperatures dropping to -288°F (-178°C)! Saturn also has many moons—over 80! Isn’t that cool?
