Fun Facts about Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun, which makes it SUPER hot during the day.

However, the crazy thing is, at night it can get really cold! It reaches temperatures we can't even withstand!

There's also another REALLY cool fact about Mercury. Did you know that a day on Mercury is longer than on Earth? Isn’t that cool? So, if you could visit Mercury, you’d have long days and even longer nights!
